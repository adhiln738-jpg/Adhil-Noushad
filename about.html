My Story

From Curious Beginner to
E-commerce Authority

My journey started over 4 years ago when I launched my first Shopify store — and lost money on bad ads and a poorly designed site. That failure became my best teacher. I became obsessed with understanding what makes an e-commerce store actually convert.

I spent thousands of hours studying top-performing Shopify stores, dissecting Meta Ads strategies, testing products across dozens of niches, and learning the data-driven way to scale.

Today, I help small business owners and entrepreneurs skip the expensive learning curve. I build premium stores and run laser-targeted ad campaigns that turn their business from struggling to scaling — profitably and predictably.
