Common Questions

How long does a Shopify store take to build?

+
Starter packages are delivered within 7 days. Growth/Pro stores take 10–14 days. Premium brand stores are scoped individually. I never sacrifice quality for speed, but I'm always fast.

Do I need a Shopify subscription to use these services?

+
Yes, Shopify starts at $39/month (USD). This is separate from my development fee. I'll guide you through the exact plan to use and help you get started.

What is the minimum recommended Meta Ads budget?

+
I recommend a minimum of ₹10,000/month for ad spend on the Starter plan. This is separate from my management fee. More budget = more data = faster results.

Do you offer refunds?

+
I don't offer refunds once work has begun, but I do offer unlimited revisions within the scope of the agreed project to ensure you're 100% happy with the result.

Can I get both a Shopify store and Meta Ads management together?

+
Absolutely — and I recommend it. When the store and ads are built together with the same strategy in mind, conversion rates are significantly higher. Contact me for a bundled quote.

How do I get started?

Simple — click any "Get Started" button or message me on WhatsApp. We'll have a free 20–30 minute strategy call, and I'll put together a custom proposal for your specific business.
